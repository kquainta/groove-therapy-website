# Groove Therapy Business Plan
**12-Month Growth Strategy (2026-2027)**

---

## Executive Summary

Groove Therapy is a Sacramento-based smooth jazz band with ambitious growth targets. This business plan outlines a 12-month strategy to:

- **Grow the mailing list** from 0 → 500+ engaged followers
- **Establish social media presence** on Facebook & Instagram with consistent, quality content
- **Achieve 2 gigs/month** through strategic local venue partnerships and corporate event bookings
- **Break into the corporate gig circuit** (conferences, private events, corporate retreats)
- **Plan and execute the inaugural Sacramento Jazz Festival** (Fall 2026 or Spring 2027)

---

## Vision

Groove Therapy becomes the go-to smooth jazz band for Sacramento venues and corporate events, known for high-quality performances, professional booking practices, and genuine fan engagement.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. **Mailing List Growth** (Owner: Lenny)
Primary channel for direct fan engagement. Focus on quality over quantity.
- Website signup form (validated, spam-resistant)
- Social media CTAs
- QR codes at venues
- Monthly newsletter automation

[See: 02_MAILING_LIST_STRATEGY.md]

### 2. **Social Media Presence** (Owner: Lenny + Kevin approval)
Build awareness and drive traffic to website/mailing list. Start with Facebook & Instagram.
- 3x/week content posting (mix of performance clips, band stories, jazz tips, upcoming gigs)
- Community engagement (respond to comments, DMs)
- Paid ads targeting Sacramento area ($50-100/month initial budget)

[See: 03_SOCIAL_MEDIA_STRATEGY.md]

### 3. **Venue Bookings** (Owner: Lenny)
Local first, occasional travel for premium gigs. Target 2 gigs/month.
- Identify and contact local venues (restaurants, bars, listening rooms, hotels)
- Develop venue relationship strategy
- Build a calendar-driven booking pipeline
- Negotiate contracts and rates

[See: 04_VENUE_BOOKING_STRATEGY.md]

### 4. **Corporate Gigs** (Owner: Lenny + Kevin)
Higher-paying events with less competition. New frontier for the band.
- Research event planners, corporate retreat companies, wedding coordinators
- Build marketing materials highlighting band's professionalism
- Network with corporate event spaces
- Consider cold outreach to local corporations

[See: 05_CORPORATE_GIG_STRATEGY.md]

### 5. **Sacramento Jazz Festival** (Owner: Kevin + Lenny)
Long-term brand builder and revenue opportunity. Requires significant planning.
- Secure venue (spring 2027 or fall 2026)
- Identify sponsors and funding
- Recruit other jazz acts
- Coordinate marketing and ticketing

[See: 06_SACRAMENTO_JAZZ_FESTIVAL_PLAN.md]

## Technology & Tools

Two key tools will be built to support this plan:

### **Mailing List System**
- Self-hosted or lightweight SaaS (e.g., Mailchimp, ConvertKit)
- Website integration with validated signup form
- Automated monthly newsletter template
- Analytics (open rates, click-through, unsubscribes)

[See: TOOL_MAILING_LIST_SPEC.md]

### **Social Media Coordination Tool**
- Content calendar (plan posts 1-2 weeks ahead)
- Approval workflow (Kevin reviews before publishing)
- Multi-platform publishing (Facebook, Instagram, potentially others)
- Analytics dashboard (engagement, reach, follower growth)
- Hashtag & best-time-to-post automation

[See: TOOL_SOCIAL_MEDIA_SPEC.md]
