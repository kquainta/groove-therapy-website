## Blockers & How to Handle Them

### "I'm not getting any gig responses"
- **Cause:** Venue decision makers are busy
- **Fix:** Increase outreach (20+ venues), improve phone pitch, offer trial gig at lower rate
- **Timeline:** Takes 2-4 weeks to hear back typically

### "Social media feels like a lot of work"
- **Cause:** Planning 8-10 posts per week is manual
- **Fix:** Plan in batches (plan 4 weeks at once), use Buffer scheduling, delegate filming to band members
- **Timeline:** Gets easier after 2-3 weeks

### "No corporate interest yet"
- **Cause:** Corporate gigs need different pitch + longer sales cycle
- **Fix:** Focus on restaurant gigs first (faster bookings), build case studies, improve demo materials
- **Timeline:** Takes 60-90 days to close first corporate deal

### "Band members aren't cooperating"
- **Cause:** Lack of clarity on goals/commitment
- **Fix:** Have band meeting, share this plan, clarify expectations, celebrate wins
- **Timeline:** Do this in Month 1
