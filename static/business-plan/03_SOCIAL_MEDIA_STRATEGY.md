## Overview

Social media is how people discover you. We're focusing on **Facebook** and **Instagram** as the core channels, with a structured content calendar and approval workflow (so Kevin reviews everything before it goes live).

## Content Pillars (What to Post About)

Create a mix of content types. Don't just promote gigs — that gets boring.

### 1. **Performance Videos & Clips** (30%)

- 15-30 second clips from recent performances
- Highlight reel from gigs
- Live session recordings
- A cappella/stripped-down versions

**Why:** Video drives 10x more engagement than text/photos alone.  
**Cadence:** 2-3x per week

**Example Posts:**
- "Vibes last Saturday at [Venue] 🎷✨ Who else was there?"
- "New arrangement of [Song] coming to our setlist next month 👇"

### 2. **Behind-the-Scenes Content** (20%)

- Band members practicing
- Pre-gig prep photos
- Studio/rehearsal candids
- Member spotlights ("Meet the bassist!" Q&A)

**Why:** People connect with people, not bands.  
**Cadence:** 1-2x per week

**Example Posts:**
- "Rehearsal mode 🎵 Working on some fresh arrangements"
- "[Member name] on the keys — been playing jazz for 15 years. Ask him anything!"

### 3. **Gig Announcements** (20%)

- "We're playing [Venue] on [Date]!"
- Ticket links/registration
- Venue highlights/directions
- "Doors open at 7, music at 8"

**Why:** This is your primary call-to-action.  
**Cadence:** 1-2x per week (more as gig date approaches)

**Example Posts:**
- "🎷 LIVE THIS FRIDAY: 8pm @ [Venue]. Smooth vibes, great drinks, better company. [GET TICKETS]"

### 4. **Educational/Inspirational Content** (15%)

- Jazz history facts ("Did you know...?")
- Music theory tips for aspiring musicians
- Motivational jazz quotes
- Musician spotlights (famous smooth jazz artists)

**Why:** Builds authority, shares value, gives people reasons to follow beyond gigs.  
**Cadence:** 1x per week

**Example Posts:**
- "Fun fact: Smooth jazz grew out of fusion in the 1970s. Artists like Grover Washington Jr. pioneered the sound 🎷"
- "If you're learning jazz, the most important thing isn't playing the notes — it's listening to what's around you. 👂"

### 5. **Calls-to-Action (CTAs)** (15%)

- "Sign up for gig announcements" → mailing list link
- "Bring a friend to our next show"
- "What's your favorite jazz artist?"
- Engagement questions ("What's your favorite Groove Therapy song?")

**Why:** These drive conversions to mailing list, build community.  
**Cadence:** Weave into other posts, not standalone

## Content Calendar

Plan your posts 1-2 weeks in advance. This takes the guesswork out and makes Kevin's approval smooth.

### Sample Weekly Structure

| Day | Content Type | Example | Notes |
|-----|--------------|---------|-------|
| **Monday** | BTS or Member Spotlight | "Meet [Member]" | Set positive tone for week |
| **Wednesday** | Educational/Inspiration | Jazz fact or quote | Midweek engagement |
| **Friday** | Gig Announcement or Video | Performance clip + upcoming gigs | Weekend mindset |
| **Sunday** | BTS or Community | Audience photo from last gig | Build connection |

**Planning Process:**
1. Lenny prepares 8-10 post ideas for the week (text + captions)
2. Kevin reviews and approves/edits
3. Lenny schedules on platform (or manual publish if platform doesn't support scheduling)
4. Post goes live at optimal time (typically Fri 6pm, Sun 10am for peak engagement)

### Tools for Scheduling

**Option 1: Native Scheduling**
- Instagram: Built-in scheduling (Business Account only)
- Facebook: Built-in scheduling
- Easy, free, integrated

**Option 2: Social Media Manager Tool**
- Buffer, Later, Hootsuite
- Can schedule to both platforms at once
- Costs $15-30/month but saves time

**Recommendation:** Start with native scheduling. If you hit 3+ posts/day, upgrade to a manager tool.

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram

- **Hashtags:** Use 15-30 relevant hashtags per post (#smoothjazz #livemusic #sacramento #groovetherapy)
- **Stories:** Post 3-5x per week (ephemeral, builds urgency)
- **Reels:** Prioritize videos over static images (IG algorithm favors Reels)
- **Captions:** 2-3 sentences max, conversational tone
- **Engagement:** Respond to every comment within 24h

### Facebook

- **Local Targeting:** Use location tags (Sacramento, Midtown, etc.)
- **Event Posts:** Use Facebook's "Event" feature for gigs (automatically reminds followers)
- **Video:** Still king — post clips natively, not YouTube links
- **Groups:** Join Sacramento music/event groups, post organically (no spam)
- **Captions:** Can be slightly longer (up to 2-3 paragraphs)

## Engagement Strategy

**Goal:** Build a community, not just broadcast.

### Respond to Comments

- **Timeline:** Within 24 hours
- **Tone:** Warm, conversational, genuine
- **Example:**
  - Comment: "Loved your set last Saturday!"
  - Response: "Thanks so much! You made the night better with your energy 🙌 Hope to see you at [next gig]"

### Respond to DMs

- **Timeline:** Within 24-48 hours
- **Process:** Direct booking inquiries to Kevin, general questions Lenny handles

- Ask questions: "What's your favorite jazz song?"
- Polls: "Should we cover this song? A) Yes B) No"
- Contests: "Tag a friend who'd love Groove Therapy — one reply wins [prize]"

## Growth Hacks (Months 3+)

### 1. **Hashtag Strategy**

Create a branded hashtag: **#GrooveTherapySounds**  
Encourage fans to use it when posting performances/reactions.

### 2. **Collaborate with Local Creators**

- Tag local venues in posts
- Partner with Sacramento musicians for cross-promotion
- Feature local artists in your stories

### 3. **Repost User Content**

When fans post about you, repost on your story/main feed. Makes them feel seen.

### 4. **Paid Ads** (Month 4+)

Start small: $50-100/month on Facebook/Instagram ads.

**Target:**
- People interested in jazz, live music
- Age 25-65
- Location: Sacramento metro
- Interest in similar bands/artists

**Goal:** Grow followers and drive traffic to mailing list.

**Expected ROI:** $1 ad spend → 2-3 new followers OR 0.5-1 mailing list signup

### 5. **Go Live**

Instagram & Facebook Live: 
- Stream from a practice/rehearsal
- Q&A with band members
- Play a short acoustic set

**Cadence:** 1x per month (low pressure to start)

## Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

| Pitfall | Problem | Solution |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Inconsistent Posting** | Followers forget you exist | Use content calendar + scheduling |
| **Only Promoting Gigs** | Boring, people unfollow | Aim for 70% value, 30% promotion |
| **Slow Response to Comments** | Kills engagement | Check socials daily, respond quickly |
| **Poor Video Quality** | Looks unprofessional | Use phone camera, good lighting, stable tripod |
| **Wrong Hashtags** | Post gets lost | Use 20-30 relevant, popular hashtags |
| **Ignoring Analytics** | Posting blind | Check what works monthly, adjust |
